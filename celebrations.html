What is the capacity of the Harbour Room and the Rooftop?

The Harbour Room can accommodate up to 200 guests for a cocktail-style event or 120 for a seated dinner. Our Rooftop space is perfect for more intimate gatherings, comfortably hosting up to 100 guests for a cocktail event or 60 for a seated function. Both spaces offer flexibility to suit various event sizes and styles.

002
002
Can we bring our own caterer or do you provide in-house catering?

We provide premium in-house catering services at the Harbour Room. Our experienced culinary team offers a wide range of menu options, from elegant canapés to multi-course gourmet dinners. We 're happy to work with you to customize menus that suit your preferences and dietary requirements. External catering is not permitted due to our commitment to maintaining the highest quality standards.

003
How far in advance should we book the venue for our wedding?

We recommend booking the Harbour Room or Rooftop at least 9-12 months in advance for weddings, especially for peak season dates (October to March). However, we occasionally have last-minute availability, so it 's always worth inquiring about your preferred date. To secure your booking, we require a signed contract and a deposit.

004
004
What makes the Harbour Room unique compared to other wedding venues in Melbourne?

The Harbour Room offers a truly unique experience with its stunning waterfront location at the Royal Melbourne Yacht Squadron. Our venue combines breathtaking views of Port Phillip Bay, versatile indoor and outdoor spaces, and exceptional service. The ability to have your ceremony, reception, and photos all in one location, along with our experienced event planning team, sets us apart from other venues in Melbourne. Plus, our rooftop space provides a picturesque setting for ceremonies or cocktail hours with unparalleled views of the Melbourne skyline.
